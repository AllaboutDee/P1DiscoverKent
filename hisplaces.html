Maidstone

Maidstone, the county town of Kent, was a major medieval market center for agriculture and the cloth trade. During the English Civil War, it saw a notable battle between Royalist forces and Parliamentary troops.

Places to Visit:

  • Leeds Castle: Often called the "loveliest castle in the world," Leeds Castle has been a royal residence since the 13th century.
  • Kent Museum of Rural Life: This museum showcases the rural lifestyle and agricultural practices that defined Kent for centuries.

Ashford

A Roman settlement that grew into a significant market town, Ashford became an industrial center in the 19th century thanks to the railway.

Places to Visit:

  • Godinton House and Gardens: A 14th-century house surrounded by beautiful gardens, showcasing historic English country life.
  • Ashford Museum: Displays artifacts tracing Ashford's transformation from Roman times to the railway era.
